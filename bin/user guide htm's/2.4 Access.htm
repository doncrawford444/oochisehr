2.4 Access

The 'Login' screen appears directly after starting the Oochis EHR application. The purpose of the 'Login' screen, is to prevent unauthorized access through password-authentication.

To log in, at the log in screen, enter a user name and password provided by your system administrators, then click, 'Enter'. The workspace (image of workspace) will appear when log in is successful.

User names and passwords must be composed of at least 6 and no more than 16 alphanumeric letters and numbers. There is a limited number of login attempts (set by the administrator in 'System' → 'Set-up') allowed before the system administrators is required to login. Also, the 'Login' screen clears itself every two minutes if no keys are pressed.

To logout, click on the 'Login' button to the left of the task bar (image of 'Login' button in the workspace).

To exit Oochis EHR, click on the 'Exit' button on the 'Login' screen. A user must logged out before exiting. Oochis EHR can be closed at any point in the work flow without altering previously recorded (or, "added") health information.

A 'Record of logins' is accessible from the 'Reports' panel of the 'Practice Assistant' window ('Practice Assistant' → 'Reports' → 'Record of logins').
