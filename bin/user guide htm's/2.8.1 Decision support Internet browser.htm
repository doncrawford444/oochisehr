2.8.1 Internet browser

'Internet browser' ('Decision Support' → 'Internet browser'):
A security-enhanced Mozilla Internet browser component is installed on the device in order to use Decision Support’s security-enhanced web-browsing encapsulation feature.

When Oochis EHR starts (upon clicking the Oochis EHR icon) the Internet browser is turned on in the background, providing instant access to the cloud when Decision Support’s ‘Browser’ button is clicked.

Due to not using commonly exploited components, some web pages may not display or function correctly, and some in-page browsing controls may not be available.

The browser’s control panel (see figure below) is simple and provides fast, intuitive browsing of Internet and open networks (any found ad hoc system of connected devices [connected via: wifi; bluetooth; ethernet; firewire; USB; etc.])
