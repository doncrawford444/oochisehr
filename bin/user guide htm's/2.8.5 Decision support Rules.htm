2.8.5 Rules

'Rules' ('Decision Support' → 'Rules'):
The Rules feature sends alerts (electronic alerts similar to emails) to users if specified conditions are met (such as recommended screenings or vaccinations).

'Rules' are heuristics, or logarithms to perturb medical information technology processes toward a specific aim, benefit, or increase in knowledge. An alert is an unexpected automated or unexpected machine-generated intervention in human work flow and reason (for the alert). Therefore, if a condition is met, that it constitutes an alert must be programmed; and the recorded conditions and attributes of the triggering event, are used in the alert to notify a responsible provider.

Example: If patients under the age of 3 and whose address is in the city, "Livingston," have not received a specific biological then alert the primary care provider to treat with shots.
