2.9.1 Patient lists

'Patient lists' ('Practice Assistant' → 'Patient lists'):

  • 'Print'
  • 'Show deleted users'
  • 'Patient lists'
The term, "Master user index," is a bit of a misnomer, as it's limited to the practice's own (computer) users, while "lists" leverages the complete records in the CHIS/EHR network (measured in units of EHR users [number and percent]).
