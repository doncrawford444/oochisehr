1.9 ONC certification criteria

Oochis EHR IS NOT (YET) AN ONC-CERTIFIED EHR SOLUTION.

There are three types of EHR solutions that the ONC certifies: ambulatory; in-patient; and both ambulatory and in-patient. Oochis EHR solution is both ambulatory and in-patient.
