2.7 Messaging

Messaging handles messages (emails and multi-media text messages), alerts (such as contraindications and messages flagged as urgent), and contact information.

Send content and pages as messages or attachments in standard formats (text, odf, doc, rtf, html, and images)*.

Send specific order items to patients, such as what prescriptions were ordered, and later, when they are filled. A dialog is displayed when an incomplete order is sent. The dialog panel choices are: 'Send incomplete'; 'Send when (the order is) completed'; and, 'Send both' (now, and when the order is completed). To send a patient laboratory results in the email, automatically when the results come in, click 'Send when completed', or, 'Send both'.
