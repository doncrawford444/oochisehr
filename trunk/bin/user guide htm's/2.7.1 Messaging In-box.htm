2.7.1 Starting and logging in

'In-box' ('Messaging' → 'In-box'):
The 'In-box' panel is used to access your email and instant messaging accounts. Received emails are displayed in the in-box display panel. Click on a message to view it's contents in the in-box (the in-box will change what it displays from a list of received messages to the message’s content). For now, use (google.gmail.com) as your email service.

For now, use (google.gmail.com) as your email service.

'Add' ('Messaging' → 'In-box' → 'Add'):
Clicking 'Add' enters the email address and text messaging number into a 'New' contact's 'Contact information' fields.

'Save' ('Messaging' → 'In-box' → 'Save'):
Click 'Save' in the displayed 'Contact information' panel to save the new contact and close the 'Contact information' panel.

'Delete' ('Messaging' → 'In-box' → 'Delete'):
Deletes the email address or message number. A dialog panel will appear warning that deleting the email address or message number is permanent and cannot be recovered.
