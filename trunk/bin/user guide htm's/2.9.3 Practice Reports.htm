2.9.3 Reports

'Reports' ('Practice Assistant' → 'Reports'):
Reports include logs such as (audit tracking reports) and diagrams for different categories of information (protected patient information, provider information, network traffic - both machine and messages passed between providers). Reports may include attributes such as items worked on, by whom, and when. Diagrams are useful in evaluating how health information is recorded and managed, while audit reports provide a means to verify events.

'Audit tracking' ('Practice Assistant' → 'Reports' → 'Audit tracking'):
An audit is a chronological record of events. Audit reports all event processes such as user-instigated actions (such as button clicks and input) and their ensuing automatic processes (such as a sequence of internal method calls that perform any [conceivable] event).
